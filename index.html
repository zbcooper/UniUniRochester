About Us

At Uni Uni, we are passionate about bringing the authentic taste and beauty of bubble tea to Rochester. Our mission is to introduce our community to a unique, refreshing, and healthy beverage experience that transcends the ordinary.

Our journey began when we discovered the incredible world of bubble tea in our travels and were inspired by its delightful flavors and colorful presentation. We knew that Rochester deserved to enjoy this fantastic beverage, and that's how Uni Uni Rochester was born.

We take pride in using only the highest quality, all-natural ingredients, with no artificial sweeteners or additives. Our teas are sourced from the finest tea estates, and we handpick our fresh fruits and toppings to ensure the ultimate taste sensation in every sip.

From classic milk teas to fruity infusions, our diverse menu is crafted to suit every taste bud, and our skilled team is dedicated to delivering an exceptional customer experience. Visit TeaRochester today and indulge in a real, beautiful, and healthy drink that will leave you craving more!
